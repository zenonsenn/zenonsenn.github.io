Boredom taints our lives. Stimulation is not a-given. Dopamine is undistributed. These traits are what befall our dearest friend, Zen. A connoisseur in the arts of creating magnificent cities that lack the financial means to support itself. He, however, has burnt out in the downfalls of modding Cities: Skylines. Now he turns to that of modded Minecraft. That is the purpose of this modpack!
